Quantum is a new generation SME focussed on serving the security needs of clients in the Western Cape. We are driven by our passion for providing exceptional levels of service, uplifting our employees through continued skills transfer and our desire to set the benchmark in the industry. Our values which we embrace are Integrity, Respect and Excellence.

Our differentials are senior management involvement in the delivery of service to our clients, being client centric in that we design our solutions according to client needs, employee focused as we believe that our employees are the most important assets of our company and digitising the functions of security officers to ensure excellence.


We are committed to developing solutions to suit the specific and unique needs of each client with a hands-on management and supervisory approach which allows clients to focus on their core business. In this way we provide a seamless, cost-effective, one-stop solution centred on the core competency of physical guarding services. We are proactive in anticipating the changing environment and the evolving needs of every one of our clients


Drawing on our extensive knowledge base, we are able to act in the capacity of security advisor and consultant when high-level risk assessments are required. Furthermore, we have the necessary expertise to facilitate the development and review of emergency preparedness plans.


We aim to partner with clients who share our values and who see security as an investment into the success of their businesses and who believe in the safety of their employees as being paramount
