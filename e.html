About Us

We believe in a hands-on approach to developing integrated solutions to meet the individual needs of our clients.

We provide seamless, cost-effective, one-stop solutions.

Quantum is a new generation SME focussed on serving the needs of clients in the Western Cape. We are driven by our passion for providing exceptional levels of service, uplifting our employees through continued skills transfer and our desire to set a benchmark in the Security Solutions industry. Our directors and management have well over 70 years of collective experience in the private security industry.


Our senior management is committed to developing solutions to suit the specific needs of each client. Our flat management structure and hands-on management approach allows clients to focus on their core business. In this way we provide a seamless, cost-effective, one- stop solution centred on the competency of physical guarding services.


We are proactive in anticipating the changing environment and evolving needs of every one of our clients. Drawing on our extensive knowledge base, we are able to act in the capacity of security advisor and consultant when high-level risk assessments are required. Furthermore, we have all the necessary expertise to facilitate the development and review of emergency preparedness plans within the areas of private and public safety
